About Me

Software Engineer & Applied Statistics

I am a Full-Stack Developer and Data Analyst with a Bachelor's degree in Applied Statistics from the University of Rwanda (Huye Campus). My unique background combines rigorous statistical training with hands-on software engineering, allowing me to build intelligent systems that leverage data for informed decision-making.

I specialize in building complete web applications using Flask on the backend and HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the frontend. On the data side, I use Python with Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn, Matplotlib and other data analysis tools to build predictive models, visualize trends, and extract meaningful insights from complex datasets. I am also highly proficient in Excel for statistical analysis, reporting, and data visualization with charts and tables.

I have built real-world systems such as Hope Collection — an online sales and delivery platform with a complete business management system that tracks attendance, worker performance, and generates statistical reports with interactive graphs and tables. I am passionate about using data to drive business growth and operational efficiency.
